About

Who I Am

Six years at the intersection of AI, HR Tech, and EdTech.

I'm Pratik — I build things with AI, and I help companies figure out what to build next. Over the last six years, I've spent most of my time at the intersection of HR Tech and EdTech, two spaces that look very different on the surface but share the same core challenge: getting the right information to the right person at the right time.

I run Perfect Skills, where the work ranges from shipping full AI products to helping founders turn a rough idea into a fundable pitch. Some weeks that means writing multi-agent systems, Other weeks it means sitting across from a client and building the business case for why their organisation needs to change. I've learned to be comfortable with both.

I've worked with solo founders, growing startups, universities, enterprise teams, and NGOs — projects as focused as a single automation script and as broad as a national-scale platform. What stays consistent is the approach: understand the problem properly, build something that actually works, and make sure the people using it can keep it running without me.
