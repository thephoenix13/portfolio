Over the last six years, I've spent most of my time at the intersection of HR Tech and EdTech, two spaces that look very different on the surface but share the same core challenge: getting the right information to the right person at the right time.

I run **Perfect Skills**, where the work ranges from shipping full AI products to helping founders turn a rough idea into a fundable pitch. Some weeks that means writing multi-agent systems and debugging WebSocket streams. What stays consistent is the approach: understand the problem properly, build something that actually works, and make sure the people using it can keep it running without me.
